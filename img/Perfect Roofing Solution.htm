About Us

Hello World

EuroAsia Products, Inc. is a privately held business based in Orlando, Florida with affiliate resources in Hong Kong and Shenzhen China. Our company was founded in 1998 by John Bowers who spent decades working for Fortune 500 companies in the USA and Europe. John served in various entrepreneurial linchpin positions including: special market sales, creative for premiums and promotional products, marketing, technical sales, buying and overseas production. He worked for companies such as: Hallmark, Graphics International, Tupperware, Le Creuset, RR Donnelly and Enercon Systems.

How are you

When many U.S. and European companies complained about the inferiority of products they manufactured abroad in the ‘80s and ‘90s, John set about teaching and training his network of factories in Western consumer product expectations, Western functional and legislative requirements, and quality standards.

In a new wORLD

For overseas manufacturing, you don’t want to learn the hard way. The potential pitfalls are many and can easily doom your products to failure. New products and brand expansions have been our business for almost two decades. EuroAsia Products is your resource for new product development and overseas manufacturing production. Our team becomes your team. You can rely on our seasoned staff to carry your ideas from concept to consumer.
